Seller Agreement & Community Policy

Welcome to Skill Connect. By registering as a seller, affiliate, or member on our platform, you agree to comply with the following terms and conditions.

SELLER REGISTRATION

To become an approved seller on Skill Connect, a one-time registration fee of ₦2,000 is required. This fee grants access to the seller dashboard, training resources, and platform opportunities.

SALES COMMISSION

Approved sellers are entitled to earn up to 30% commission on eligible course sales completed through their referral links or assigned sales channels, subject to platform policies.

SELLER REFERRAL REWARD

Members can qualify for a ₦50,000 bonus after successfully referring 100 active sellers who complete registration and meet Skill Connect's verification requirements.

COURSE SALES ACHIEVEMENT BONUS

Members can qualify for a ₦150,000 performance bonus after successfully generating 100 verified course sales in accordance with Skill Connect's tracking system and payment policies.

COURSE PRICING

All courses listed on Skill Connect must maintain the minimum approved pricing established by the company. Courses below ₦10,000 may not be eligible for seller commissions or promotional programs.

WITHDRAWAL POLICY

Withdrawals are processed exclusively on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays. The minimum withdrawal amount is ₦1000, and the maximum withdrawal limit is up to ₦5,000,000 per transaction.

ANTI-FRAUD POLICY

Skill Connect maintains a zero-tolerance policy against fraud, deception, identity theft, fake registrations, payment manipulation, chargeback abuse, account farming, or any activity intended to unfairly gain financial benefits.

ACCOUNT VERIFICATION

Skill Connect reserves the right to verify seller identities, suspend suspicious accounts, request supporting documentation, and investigate unusual activities at any time.

FAIR PARTICIPATION

All members are expected to conduct themselves professionally and respectfully. Harassment, threats, discrimination, hate speech, or abusive behavior toward other members will not be tolerated.

NO GUARANTEED INCOME

Skill Connect provides educational tools, sales opportunities, and referral programs. Individual earnings depend on personal effort, skill, marketing activities, and market conditions.

ACCURATE INFORMATION

Members must provide accurate registration details. False information may result in account suspension or permanent removal from the platform.

PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

  • Fake referrals
  • Multiple accounts for the same person
  • Spam marketing
  • Misleading advertisements
  • Unauthorized use of Skill Connect branding
  • Fraudulent transactions
  • Manipulation of platform systems

PAYMENT REVIEW

Skill Connect reserves the right to review and validate transactions before commissions, bonuses, or rewards are approved and paid.

PLATFORM CHANGES

Skill Connect may update pricing, commission structures, bonuses, features, or platform policies when necessary. Members will be notified of significant updates.

ACCOUNT TERMINATION

Skill Connect reserves the right to suspend or terminate any account that violates these policies or engages in activities that harm the company, its members, or customers.

COMMUNITY VALUES

Skill Connect is committed to providing equal opportunities to all members regardless of gender, ethnicity, nationality, disability, religion, or background. We welcome honest individuals who are committed to learning, growth, and ethical business practices.

ACCEPTANCE OF TERMS

By clicking "I Agree" and registering on Skill Connect, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and accepted this Seller Agreement and Community Policy.
